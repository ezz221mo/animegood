one piece

One Piece is a Japanese anime television series produced by Toei Animation that premiered on Fuji TV in October 1999. It is based on Eiichiro Oda's manga series of the same name. The story follows the adventures of Monkey D. Luffy, a boy whose body gains the properties of rubber after accidentally eating a Devil Fruit.

naruto

The Naruto manga revolves around ninja stories, including the story of the series' hero, Uzumaki Naruto. This world created by manga artist Kishimoto Masashi has its own unique history and culture. The world of Naruto is divided into 5 main nations: Fire, Water, Wind, Lightning, and Earth. Each country is ruled by a noble called a feudal ninja
